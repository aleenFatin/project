WHY CHOOSE SHELTER?

SHELTER is a registered welfare organization that helps abused, abandoned, neglected or at-risk children. We provide care to them and raise public awareness on child abuse and protection.

SHELTER provides food and financial aid to families. Its purpose is to preserve the family unit by providing the basic needs for the children such as food, financial assistance for tuition fees, schools fees, school bus expenses, etc.
